About Faguni Farms

Faguni Farms is a tech-driven, mission-led organic farming initiative based in Chandela Village, Bilaspur, Uttar Pradesh. We grow and supply high-quality, chemical-free dairy and agricultural products, directly from our farm to your facility.

With our zero-adulteration promise, transparent sourcing, and farm-to-table traceability, we help businesses, hotels, restaurants, institutions, and residential societies switch to clean, ethical, and affordable food options—cutting out the middlemen and ensuring farmer-first pricing.
