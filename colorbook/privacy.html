Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to our Kids Coloring Book & Games app. We take your privacy, and especially the privacy of children, very seriously. This policy explains our practices regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All coloring data, drawings, and game progress remain locally on your device.

3. Local Storage

We use local storage on your device only to save your basic app preferences, such as your selected language. This data is never sent to any external server.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising frameworks that collect user data.

5. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal data, our app is completely safe for children and fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar global regulations.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
